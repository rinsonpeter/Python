IPHONE 12 & 12 PRO PRICE REVEALED

Prajjwal Ekhande May 05, 2020
There is a lot of discussion in the market about the upcoming 12 series of iPhone that the launch of the device has been postponed due to Coronavirus. In this, some such reports are also coming in which leaks about this upcoming series are also coming out. In a post on Twitter, Jon Prosser has leaked the specification and price of this series.

iPhone 12 

Through a YouTube video and tweet, he has revealed a lot of information related to the iPhone 12 lineup. According to the claim, it has leaked through a reliable source. Also, given the tipster's past record, the leak may prove to be true.

Been seeing some reports speculating on iPhone 12 prices, so I research on it 👇

iPhone 12 series all models price

1).  5.4 iPhone 12 D52G
      OLED / 5G
      2 cam
      $ 649

2).  6.1 iPhone 12 D53G
      OLED / 5G
      2 cam
      $ 749

3).  6.1 iPhone 12 Pro D53P
      OLED / 5G
      3 cam + LiDAR
      $ 999

4).  6.7 iPhone 12 Pro Max D54P
      OLED / 5G
      3 cam + LiDAR
      $ 1,099

As far as the processor is concerned, Apple has codenamed iPhone 12, 12 Pro, and iPhone 12 Pro Max as D52G, D53G, D53P, D54P.

Short Specification of all iPhone 12 series models

In most basic models, you can see a 5.4-inch / 6.1-inch OLED screen. Both models will be offered with 5G connectivity and dual camera setup. They can be priced at around $ 649 and $ 749.

Apart from this, in the iPhone 12 Pro, you can get a display with 6.1-inch LiDAR support. The triple camera can also be seen on the backside. According to leaks, this model can be priced at $ 999.

The iPhone 12 Pro Max D54P will be the top variant of this series, in which the company is going to provide large display, 5G support, triple camera setup with LiDAR support. It may cost $ 1099.

If you look at the price, if it proves to be true, the price has been kept much better here because you will also get 5G support in them. If you look back, the 5G model of Galaxy S20 proved to be 20% more expensive than the normal 4G model.
